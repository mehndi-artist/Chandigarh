Bridal Mehndi Guidelines

Pre-Application Tips:

  • Get your manicure and pedicure done before the mehndi application.
  • Ensure your hands and legs are clean and free of body lotions or oils.
  • Wear comfortable clothing with short or easily foldable sleeves.

Post-Application Care for a Dark Stain:

  • Keep the paste on for a minimum of 6 to 8 hours (overnight is best).
  • Apply a mixture of lemon juice and sugar to seal the dried henna.
  • Avoid water contact on the applied areas for at least 12 hours after scraping off the paste.
  • Apply natural mustard oil, eucalyptus oil, or Vicks VapoRub for heat generation.
